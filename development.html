Tredway Riverfront Park - Development Plans

The township has a grant to surface the trail from the Freeport Bridge north to the Kiskiminetas Bridge. This should occur in summer of 2024. They have also let a contract to log the area near the Kiski Bridge to develop a township park.

Armstrong Trail is working on the trail from Rosston to Schenley and the Kiskiminetas Bridge and hopes to have that section completed by December 2023. At which point there will be an illegal crossing of the on-grade railroad tracks to connect to the Tredway Trail.

The Armstrong Trail is also working to complete the trail from Schenley to Leechburg and is expected to by finished by the beginning of the summer 2024.

The township, along with Westmoreland County is in the planning stage of an overhead crossing of the Norfolk Southern tracks by the Kiskiminetas Bridge to replace the illegal on-grade crossing.
